25 R&B Albums That Deserve a Listen

By Julianna Mandarino, Oct 19, 2021

Whether or not you are a fan of R&B music, if you listen to music, there's no escaping this genre's versatility and wide spread reach. With artists like SZA and Frank Ocean who dominate within newer R&B, Usher and Aaliayah who have made their mark on 90's R&B, and Michael Jackson and Aretha Franklin who have become living legends, there's no denying the talent that the artists within this genre posess.
