IGOR: My Favorite Album

By Julianna Mandarino, Oct 28, 2021

IGOR is an album, written, produced, and sung by Tyler, The Creator. It tells a full length story of a relationship starting from the beggining of it all the way to the end. This album has impeccable and experimental production, a lot of different sounds and singing styles, and a great story to tie it all together. No doubt this album is timesless and will always be a masterpiece.

Ungodly Hour: A Recent Obsession

By Julianna Mandarino, Oct 25, 2021

Ungodly Hour, an album by Chloe X Halle is an underrated R&B album. These two sisters have such different tones of voice and styles of singing, but somehow their harmonies and voices generally mesh so well together. All their songs ae unlike the others and that variability is what makes a good album. It's fun, vibrant, has good beats and overall needs more recognition from fans of R&B music.

Listen To Ungodly Hour

Janelle Monet: An Artist Who is Underrated

By Julianna Mandarino, Oct 20, 2021

One word to decribe Janelle Monet: ICON. This women is a role model to so many women around the world, incuding myself. Being a black, queer singer, who sings about what she feels needs to be said is amazing. She always has important messages within her lyrics or just generally in the things she puts her name on. I think she needs to be more recognized within pop culture and society nowadays than she is right now, and her music is top teir quality.

Sawayama: My Ultimate Pump Up Album

By Julianna Mandarino, Oct 17, 2021

Rina Sawayama is another underrated artist that I think deserves more recognition. This album is so fun and I love to drive around in the car and just dance and scream the lyrics to this album. She even got recognition from THE Elton John when he asked to hop on her song Chosen Family. I think the vibrancy of this album is really cool and wavy. She is still getting started and I can't wait to hear what she has next for us.
